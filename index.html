About Us
"The more you are aware of yourself, the happier you will be"

In the ever-changing landscape that is our student life, mental health needs to be a top priority. This is where Saathi Club comes in. We are a mental health support group that collaborates with the Center for Holistic Well-Being to offer a safe space to anyone in need and ensure their smooth journey while at IITG.

Activities
• Peer Mentorship -

In the ever-changing landscape that is our student life, mental health needs to be a top priority. This is where Saathi Club comes in. We are a mental health support group that collaborates with the Center for Holistic Well-Being to offer a safe space to anyone in need and ensure their smooth journey while at IITG.

• Ice Breaking Session -

Talking about mental health is not so easy in the society. In order to make people feel at ease to talk to counsellors, counsellors from Centre for Holistic well being IITG visit each hostel to spend quality time and have fun with the students through various activities and games.

• One-on-one Interaction session to freshers -

Is done in order to get familiar with counselling process.

• Declutter -

Fancy a little less involvement in the counselling process but have questions?? You can drop them in a G form and those will answered by the counsellors. Sensitive questions are replied privately and general questions are circulated as a periodical. So, you'll get benefitted even without asking anything directly!
